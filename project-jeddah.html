Featured Project

Jeddah Waterfront Pavilion

Jeddah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Project Overview

The Jeddah Waterfront Pavilion stands as a testament to the harmonious relationship between architecture and nature. Positioned along the pristine shores of the Red Sea, this cultural landmark celebrates Jeddah's maritime heritage while offering a contemporary space for community gathering and artistic expression.

Drawing inspiration from the traditional dhow boats that once sailed these waters, the pavilion's flowing forms and undulating roof create a dynamic interplay between land and sea. The structure's organic geometry responds to the coastal winds and tides, creating an ever-changing relationship with its environment throughout the day.

Project Details

Location: Jeddah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia
Year: 2022
Area: 12,500 sq meters
Type: Cultural Pavilion
Status: Completed
Client: Jeddah Municipality
