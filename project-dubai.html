Featured Project

The Dubai Vertical Garden

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Project Overview

The Dubai Vertical Garden represents a revolutionary approach to sustainable urban living in one of the world's most dynamic cities. This residential tower redefines the relationship between architecture and nature, creating a living ecosystem that rises gracefully into the desert sky while maintaining deep connections to the earth below.

Each floor of the tower features extensive green terraces that spiral around the building's core, creating a continuous garden that filters air, provides shade, and creates microclimates throughout the structure. The integration of traditional Arabic courtyard principles with vertical farming technology creates a new paradigm for desert architecture.

Project Details

Location: Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Year: 2024
Area: 45,000 sq meters
Type: Residential Tower
Status: In Design
Client: Emaar Properties
